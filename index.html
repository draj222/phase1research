why us?

In today's day and age, research is a fundamental skill that every college applicant and member of the workforce must have. Finding affordable research is a challenging task these days, and Phase 1 has a goal to help with that as well, offering affordable services. Phase 1's mission is to provide research services for students with little to no research experience in order to equip them with skills needed to succeed. Phase 1 serves as the first step, an introductory approach, for students to get exposed to research. With mentors studying at Top 20 universities, students will be able to learn from these experienced individuals, as well as get guidance with research projects and/or papers.
