why us?

In today's day and age, research is a fundamental skill that every college applicant and member of the workforce must have. Phase 1 has a goal of providing every student with access to free resources, and match them with a mentor from a T20 university and/or from the workforce.

Additionally, Phase 1 realizes that there are students around the world who can't afford resources, ultimately leading to the lack of exposure to quality education. Phase 1 supports students in underpriveleged areas with education such as curriculums, as well as supporting them with tangible resources such as laptops and WiFi routers.
